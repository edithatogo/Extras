# Track Spec: DroidVNC-NG No-Admin Packaging

## Goal
Document why DroidVNC-NG is not a first-class no-admin desktop candidate in this repo and keep collision tracking intact.

## Scope
- Contract: `packaging/contracts/droidvnc-ng.json`

## Acceptance Criteria
- Candidate is either blocked with explicit reason(s), or only progressed when a validated desktop-side replacement is present.
- Article dependency (`VNC client` + `Android server`) is recorded for any future migration plan.
